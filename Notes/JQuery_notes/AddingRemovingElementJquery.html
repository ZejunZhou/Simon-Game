Adding and Removing Elemnt Jquery

Add method: before, after, append, prepend 
$("h1").before("
Hello world
")
  • Before: adding directly before the selected target (h1)
  • After: adding directly after the selected target (h1)
  • prepend: adding inside before the selected target (h1)
  • append: adding inside after the selected target (h1)
Remove 
$("h1").remove()
